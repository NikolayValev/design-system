# Operating Metrics

Track these metrics weekly to keep execution honest.

## Delivery

- Lead time for change
- Deployment frequency
- Change failure rate
- Mean time to recovery

## Product Engineering

- Story cycle time (planned vs delivered)
- Escaped defects by app
- Build stability (% successful CI runs)
- Contract break incidents (design-system/auth/state)

## Platform

- Terraform drift incidents
- Failed deploy rollbacks
- Shared package upgrade adoption time across apps

## Target Cadence

- Review every Friday
- Monthly trend review and corrective actions
